Dice

The size of the canvas here is 1024 x 768. As many complete dice as possible are rendered onscreen. Roll all of them by clicking the screen! The total of all of them is displayed at the bottom of the canvas.

Press the A key to increase the size of the dice, and the S key to decrease the size of the dice. The D key resets the size of the dice to 20. The number of dice onscreen is displayed at the bottom of the canvas, to the right of the total dots.

The maximum number of legible dice is 7838 with each die at size 10x10. This number excludes the dice covered by the displayed text.
